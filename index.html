Beauty

Loboc River is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Bohol. Since pre-Hispanic times, the Loboc River has played an important role in the lives of the locals. Please tell your friends about us. Thank you.

The source of the Loboc River is located in the town of Carmen, almost in the center of Bohol. From Carmen, the river takes a westerly course for a distance of about 1.5 kilometres (1 mi) then flows due south into the Mindanao or Bohol Sea.

Flood History

The Loboc River overflowed its bank on 1847. Next record of overflowing was on November 26, 1876 inflicting much damage to properties and human lives.

Subsequent flooding occurs in November 21, 1953, and November 19 to 20, 1964. It has been observed that a period of 8 to 9 years usually elapse before the next flooding.
